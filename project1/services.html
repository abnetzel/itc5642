Training

Depending on your dog's development level, I will work with you to develop a training plan. I have been certified by the North Carolina Canine Training Institute to train dogs on basic obedience. Training sessions are typically 15-20 minutes in length; however, I request access to your dog 3-4 times per week to build consistency. As your dog's obedience level increases, I will work with you to make sure you are familiar with commands and are comfortable handling your dog.
