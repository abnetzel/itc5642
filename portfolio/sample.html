About Me

I'm a Wisconsin native who grew up in North Carolina, andI hold a bachelor's degree in psychology, a master's degree in education, and have 14 years' experience in the education field. My experience in developing learning content and interacting with students spans the classroom environment, private online industry, and freelance opportunities. Over the past few years, I've has supplemented my instructional and curriculum design experience with video producation and interactive development to provide learning content in multiple formats. I currently work at NC State's Industry Expansion Solutions as an Instructional Designer, and prior to my arrival at IES, I served as an Accessibility Technologist for Wake Technical Community College, where I served as a college-wide resource and trainer on W3C accessible web content. I am very happy to have joined the ASU family and to have begun studies toward an M.A. in Educational Media-Instructional Technology.
