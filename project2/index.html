What is Office Mix?

Office Mix is a free extention to PowerPoint that makes it easy to turn your PowerPoint presentations into interactive online lessons. It features high-fidelity animations, audio-video narration, free-form inking, hyperlink capabilities, and quizzes and polls. Watch the video below to learn more about Office Mix's features.
