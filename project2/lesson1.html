Office Mix Works with Office 2016 and Office 365

 

  1. To begin, download office mix from mix.office.com
  2. Click the Get Office Mix link
    • sign in using your office 365 account or
    • sign in with a Microsoft account
  3. Cick the "Get Office Mix" button to download
  4. When the image below appears, click "I agree to the license terms" then click install

  5. Download starts automatically
  6. You are ready to start using Mix
